Klim

Klim is milk spelled backwards. It is also the name of one of the world's leading type foundries. Kris (the owner) wanted us to create a new way for his customers to try out his fonts. We came up with a simple and elegant tool where customers can create custom specimens using a mixture of fonts, and share them with friends.
